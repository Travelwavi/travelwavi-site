Materials for journalists, bloggers, and partners covering TravelWavi.

One-line description

TravelWavi is a community-powered iOS travel companion app: real tips from real travelers in 100+ countries, AI-powered planning, and 11 built-in tools — all in one place, in 20+ languages.

The story

TravelWavi was built by Avi Yair, a solo Israeli founder, after years of travelers' frustration with stale guidebooks and incentive-driven review platforms. The thesis: travelers' best advice comes from other travelers — not editorial teams or sponsored placements. Combined with low-cost AI summarization and a freely available 20-language localization, TravelWavi turns the global community of travelers into a real-time, multilingual local guide.
